How to Play

This is a standard game of chess. The goal is to checkmate your opponent's king.

Controls:

  • Click on a piece to select it, then click on a valid square to move it.
  • Alternatively, you can drag and drop pieces to their destination.
  • Use the buttons below the board to start a new game, take back a move, resign, or get help.
